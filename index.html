NetherB0T is a multi-server Minecraft bot and shop system.

Supported servers:

  • 8b8t
  • 6b6t
  • 2b2t
  • Jonarchy
  • QuiltAnarchy

Orders are created here, pasted into a Discord ticket, and then fulfilled in-game.

After delivery, use *kit in-game (on any supported server) to receive your kits. Do not disconnect, as the bot will continue automatically teleporting to you until all kits are fully delivered using /tpa.

How it works:

  1. Join the NetherB0T Discord (Kosborei) and open a ticket at the top of the page.
  2. Go to the Shop section and select all the kits you want to purchase.
  3. Click copy, then paste the order into your ticket.
  4. A staff member will manually verify your order and input it into the bot.
  5. After completing payment to the provided crypto address for the exact price (generated using !paygen), you will be added to the kitbot whitelist.
  6. The bot uses AI pathfinding (Baritone) to retrieve items from the kit stash and automatically /tpa to you, delivering every kit without any human needing to travel to you.

(If you end up not receiving the kits then just ping @neca970 i'll manually transport it to you as a refund, and give up to 10$ of store credit)
